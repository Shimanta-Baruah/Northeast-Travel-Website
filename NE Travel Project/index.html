Adventure Activities

Thrilling experiences await in the Northeast

Trekking

Explore mountain trails, from easy walks to challenging high-altitude treks

  • Dzukou Valley Trek (Nagaland)
  • Goecha La Trek (Sikkim)
  • Living Root Bridges Trek (Meghalaya)

River Rafting

Navigate through exciting rapids and pristine river systems

  • Teesta River (Sikkim)
  • Brahmaputra River (Assam)
  • Rangeet River (Sikkim)

Wildlife Safari

Encounter rare wildlife in pristine national parks and sanctuaries

  • Kaziranga National Park (Assam)
  • Namdapha National Park (Arunachal)
  • Manas National Park (Assam)

Paragliding

Soar through the skies with breathtaking aerial views

  • Gangtok (Sikkim)
  • Shillong (Meghalaya)
  • Kohima (Nagaland)

Caving

Explore mysterious underground caves and limestone formations

  • Mawsmai Cave (Meghalaya)
  • Arwah Cave (Meghalaya)
  • Krem Liat Prah (Meghalaya)

Camping

Sleep under the stars in pristine natural settings

  • Tso Lhamo Lake (Sikkim)
  • Umiam Lake (Meghalaya)
  • Sela Pass (Arunachal)
